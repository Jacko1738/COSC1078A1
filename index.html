PROJECT IDEA

For my idea, the game would take place during the medieval era. There will be two castles on opposing sides of the map (one for each team). Three lanes will connect the two castles and between the lanes will be areas for the players to collect resources like wood and stone. After a team has collect a certain number of materials, they can build a siege weapons (for example, 1 wood and 1 stone creates a catapult) which will increase the line of attack closer towards the enemy castle; this line does not have any impact on the game other than the win condition – the one that reaches the enemy’s castle wins. The areas connecting the lanes allow for you to help your fellow teammates in other adjacent lanes. The combat system will heavily rely on skill or mechanics where certain classes will gain a slight advantage over another, for example the spear class will have a longer range and will easily land attacks on the axe class whereas the sword class can easily block the attack with their shield. Same with the axe class who combats the sword class as they can break their shield but weak against the spear class. However, having a class that counters your opponent does not mean a certain win as it runs down to the timing and order (combos) of your attacks. Different modes will be available for how long you wish the game to be. For example, the ‘normal’ mode is the full map whereas the ‘quick play’ mode is half the map and will allow for a much shorter game. Controls will consist of the basic controls of any MOBA where abilities are used by q, w, e and r and the mouse is used to move the character around the map. An example of the abilities of each class could be:
Sword & shield Axe Spear
Q – slash Q – slash Q – slash
W – block (able to block any attack) W – slam (able to still to damage to sword class when used ‘W’ ability)) W – spin (pushes enemies away for you)
E – lunge E – Throw (throws axe on chain) E - stab
R – Rampage – constant slashing for x seconds – consistent damage R –blood rage – allows all attacks to go through sword class’ ’W’ ability and grants increased shield R – Throws spear
As you can see the ‘Q’ and ‘E’ abilities are quite similar for all classes and ‘W’ and ‘R’ abilities are unique to the class, that will help counter one of the other classes. This example will obviously need to be tweaked and cooldowns and amount of damage has not been added yet since it will be much easier to figure out when messing around in an early stage of the game. Having these basic abilities and few classes (characters, heroes, champions) will hopefully fix the problem that many MOBAs have of having broken champions where skill is not a factor in winning the game which causes undesirable feelings. To develop my project idea a free game engine like Unity or Unreal Engine (most likely unity since that is what I am familiar with) would be useful as it provides a lot of work and resources that you would usually use to make a unique game engine for your game. Depending on how intense the game you are making in Unity the better computer you will need, for my idea any modern-day laptop should be able to run Unity to develop the game described in my idea. Although you may already have a laptop and able to download Unity you will still need to be able to code, especially in the language of C#.
